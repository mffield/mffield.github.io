Matthew Field is an engineer and researcher working across artificial intelligence, medical image analysis, machine learning, robotics, and data science.

He is the Director of **AI Medicine and Engineering** and an Adjunct Research Fellow with the **University of New South Wales** and the **University of Wollongong**. His current work sits at the intersection of AI, medicine, and engineering, with a particular focus on federated learning, healthcare data systems, oncology prediction models, and medical imaging.

## Research areas

- Federated learning and privacy-preserving analytics
- Medical image analysis and radiotherapy data science
- Machine learning for cancer prediction and decision support
- Robotics, dynamical systems, and control
- Engineering automation and software development

## Current and recent appointments

- **Director**, AI Medicine and Engineering (Jul 2023–present)
- **Postdoctoral Research Fellow**, University of New South Wales (Feb 2017–Jun 2023)
- **Postdoctoral Research Fellow**, University of Wollongong (Jan 2016–Feb 2017)
- **Postdoctoral Research Fellow**, University of Sydney (Sep 2014–Jan 2016)

## Education

- **PhD, Artificial Intelligence**, University of Wollongong, 2015
- **BEng, Electrical Engineering (First Class Honours)**, University of Wollongong, 2006
